Example websites for analysis on UX and UI

Exclusive Books

Exclusive Books Online

Exclusive Books is a well-known South African bookselling chain which incorporates both physical stores as well as online purchasing options.

Pros:

  • There is a quick loading time between each page to easily and quickly look at a variety of books.

Cons:

  • The navigation bar uses a pointer cursor for some list headings which you cannot go to such as the “Young Adult” section, however for the “Manga” section it is available to view.
  • The spacing of book images are not constant.
  • The spacing between book images in different pages are different.

Ster-Kinekor

Ster-Kinekor

Ster-Kinekor is a South African cinema company which shows the currently released movies, trailers, and showtimes.

Pros:

  • The website has a good responsive design to different devices.
  • The contrast of text to the background colour makes it easy to read.

Cons:

  • The navigation bar doesn't indicate to the user which page they are currently viewing.
  • There is no indication of the current movie showings at any cinema.
  • The website often has issues where the movies are not visible such as this instance on the 30 March 2025 and the previous weeks in early March of this year.

Garmin

Garmin Official Site | South Africa

Garmin is an American smartwatch company which offers a range of sport and smartwatches. The main products which are advertised are the smartwatches and the sportwatches. The company also sells products in the automotive category such as dash cameras and Global Positioning System (GPS) devices.

Pros:

  • Good responsive design.
  • Breadcrumbing for navigation.
  • Filtering by versions and features on each page.

Cons:

  • A lot of the text on each page is in capital letters
  • The hover effect on the homepage cards is not consistent on other pages.

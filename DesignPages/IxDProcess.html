Overall Goal for my Website

The overall goal that I have for my website is to create a minimalistic style while showcasing a few advanced technical skills on the website. I want to keep each page of my website minimal where a few select images and text blocks are shown at a time. This will encourage potential job recruiters as well as general users to gain more insight into who I am by focusing on one item at a time.
